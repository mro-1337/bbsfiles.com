"DoorFrame v2.1 for Visual Basic for DOS v1.0"
"DoorFrame v4.9 for  QB(tm) v4.5"
"DoorFrame v3.3 for BASCOM/PDS v7.1 "
------------------------------------------
DoorFrame utilizes some of the routines
contained in Tom Hanlin's PBClone library.
The COM routines are handled by the
QBSerial I/O library. DoorFrame Doors
development library has direct support for
most BBS Softwares and it supports COM
0 - 15, up to 115K Baud, non-standard
IRQ's multi-nodes, music, high score
bulletin, box and line drawing, ANSI and
ASCII file send. Also supports multi-port
boards using a FOSSIL including
PCBoard/M. Also detects and reports the
presence of RIPscrip on the remote.
